# Aurora Bank Financial Analysis
## Project Overview
The Aurora Banking institution aims to gain insights into customer behavior, transaction patterns, and financial performance. This analysis provides a comprehensive understanding of the bank's operations. By examining the financial data, the bank can identify areas for improvement. The goal is to inform business decisions with data-driven insights.

## Business Objectives
- To assess the performance and usage of different card types and brands to inform future product development and service enhancements
- To Analyze financial profiles and credit card usage patterns to predict the likelihood of default, enabling more accurate credit limit assignments and loan approvals.
- To track and evaluate customer spending habits across different merchant categories, locations, and transaction types to gain insights into consumer preferences and lifestyle trends.
- To explore regional trends in spending and financial behavior by analyzing transaction data in relation to geographic indicators such as city, and state.
- To identify unusual or suspicious transaction behaviors using chip usage patterns, error logs, and location data to enhance fraud prevention mechanisms.
